| living upon the history

This project explores the juxtaposition of two distinct worlds: the futuristic, uniform Cube City suspended above Earth to escape relentless earthquakes, and a vibrant, close-knit community on Earth's surface, emphasizing themes of memory, identity, and the human connection to place.
